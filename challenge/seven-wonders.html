The Mausoleum at Halicarnassus

The Mausoleum A grand tomb built for Mausolus, satrap of Caria. at Halicarnassus was one of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, built around 350 BCE in modern-day Turkey. It stood approximately 45 meters high and combined Greek, Egyptian, and Lycian architectural styles.
